📊 # Employee Data Analysis – Problem Statements
<b>
📌 ## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee data to uncover insights related to salary distribution, performance, login behavior, and workforce composition. The goal is to simulate real-world business questions using SQL.

🔥 ## Problem Statements  
🧑‍💼 1. Workforce Composition Analysis

Problem Statement:
Analyze the distribution of employees based on gender and team to understand workforce diversity and team structure.

💰 2. Salary Distribution & Pay Gap Analysis

Problem Statement:
Examine salary distribution across different genders and teams to identify any pay disparities or imbalances within the organization.

🎯 3. Bonus Allocation Insights

Problem Statement:
Evaluate how bonuses are distributed among employees and determine whether bonus allocation is influenced by salary, team, or senior management status.

👨‍💼 4. Senior Management Impact Analysis

Problem Statement:
Compare salaries, bonuses, and login behavior between senior management and non-senior employees to understand their impact and engagement levels.

⏱️ 5. Employee Login Behavior Analysis

Problem Statement:
Analyze employees' last login times to identify working patterns, peak activity hours, and potential productivity trends.

📅 6. Employee Joining Trends

Problem Statement:
Study employee start dates to identify hiring trends over time and determine periods of high recruitment activity.

🏢 7. Team Performance & Compensation Analysis

Problem Statement:
Analyze team-wise salary and bonus distribution to identify high-performing or high-cost teams within the organization.

⚖️ 8. Gender-Based Bonus & Salary Comparison

Problem Statement:
Investigate whether there is any significant difference in salary and bonus allocation between male and female employees.

📊 9. High Earners & Top Performers Identification

Problem Statement:
Identify top-paid employees and analyze whether higher salaries correlate with higher bonuses or senior management roles.

🔍 10. Employee Engagement vs Compensation

Problem Statement:
Examine the relationship between employee login activity and their salary/bonus to understand if higher-paid employees are more engaged.
